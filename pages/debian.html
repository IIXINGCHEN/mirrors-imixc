使用说明

根据对应的debian版本，编辑/etc/apt/sources.list文件, 修改为如下内容。（操作前请做好相应备份）

buster

deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster-updates main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster-updates main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster-backports main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ buster-backports main contrib non-free

stretch

deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch-updates main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch-updates main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch-backports main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ stretch-backports main contrib non-free

jessie

deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie-updates main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie-updates main contrib non-free
deb http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie-backports main contrib non-free
deb-src http://mirrors.imixc.top/debian/ jessie-backports main contrib non-free
